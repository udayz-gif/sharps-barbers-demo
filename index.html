— The craft

Eleven years.
One obsession.

Sharps started in a single chair on Wellington Street. No website, no fancy signage — just a barber who'd spent fifteen years learning his trade in London and wanted to bring something proper back to Slough.

Three chairs later, we're still doing it the same way: slow, careful, considered. Every cut starts with a conversation. Every shave ends with a smile.
